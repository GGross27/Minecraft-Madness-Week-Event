<Tour-Guide> About the Event

Minecraft Madness Weekend is an exciting, action-packed event designed for gamers, streamers, and YouTubers to come together and play Minecraft competitively over a weekend. Regardless of experience level, participants will enjoy a series of challenges ranging from peaceful to hardcore difficulty, with a focus on fun and friendly competition.

The event is open to anyone aged 16+ and will be monitored to ensure a safe and respectful online environment for all players. Users can access the event's challenges, download mod packs, and track their progress on the live leaderboard via the website, making it the perfect place for anyone looking to dive into a weekend of Minecraft madness.

🎮 Minecraft Madness Weekend Event Schedule

📅 Saturday & Sunday | ⚔️ 3 Events Per Day | 🏆 3-4 Challenges Per Event

📅 Day 1: Saturday - Classic & Adventure Challenges

🌿 Event 1: Peaceful – Parkour, Building & Puzzle Challenges

1️⃣ 🏃 Parkour Race – Speedrun a complex parkour course.
2️⃣ 🛠 Build Battle – Players build structures based on a surprise theme.
3️⃣ 🧩 Puzzle Dungeon – Solve redstone and lever puzzles to escape.
4️⃣ 📦 Block Shuffle – Players must find and stand on a random block before time runs out.

⚔️ Event 2: Adventure – PvE & Exploration Challenges

1️⃣ 🏝️ Treasure Hunt – Solve riddles to find hidden loot chests.
2️⃣ 🏹 Mob Arena – Survive increasingly difficult waves of mobs.
3️⃣ 🌊 Ocean Monument Raid – Defeat Guardians & Elder Guardians.
4️⃣ ⚔️ Raid Defense – Protect a village from an intense Pillager raid.

🔥 Event 3: Hard – PvP & Endgame Challenges

1️⃣ 💣 TNT Run – Avoid falling into the void as the ground disappears.
2️⃣ 🐉 Ender Dragon Battle Royale – Fight teams while battling the Ender Dragon.
3️⃣ 🪓 Kit PvP Arena – PvP with customized gear kits.
4️⃣ 👻 Haunted Mansion Infiltration – Sneak into a mansion full of traps and enemies.

📅 Day 2: Sunday - Chaos, Strategy & PvP Showdowns

🌾 Event 1: Peaceful – Farming, Redstone & Collection Challenges

1️⃣ 🐑 Farming Frenzy – Grow crops and breed animals the fastest.
2️⃣ 🔴 Redstone Puzzle Race – Complete a Redstone circuit fastest.
3️⃣ 🎣 Fishing Derby – Catch rare fish within a time limit.
4️⃣ 🚛 Item Hunt – Collect random items as fast as possible.

🏗️ Event 2: Adventure – Escape & Strategy Challenges

1️⃣ 🔐 Minecraft Escape Room – Solve puzzles to escape a locked area.
2️⃣ 🧭 Maze Madness – Navigate a massive, changing labyrinth.
3️⃣ 🎯 Archery Duel – Hit moving targets with obstacles.
4️⃣ 🗺️ The Great Nether Race – Race in the Nether using limited resources.

⚡ Event 3: Hard – Ultimate Survival & Chaos Challenges

1️⃣ ☁️ Skyblock Deathmatch – Battle on floating islands.
2️⃣ 🔥 Apocalypse Mode – Survive mobs, firestorms, and TNT.
3️⃣ 🏆 UHC Deathmatch – Hardcore PvP with no health regen.
4️⃣ ⚡ Lightning Round PvP – Fast-paced, high-speed PvP.
